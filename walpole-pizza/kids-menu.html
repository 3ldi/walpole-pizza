Enjoy our kids menu

Discover the flavors of Italy, through our traditional and new recipes.

Margherita - The Origins $8.50

Pizza Margherita is a typical Neapolitan pizza, made with San Marzano tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, salt, and extra-virgin olive oil. This Pizza has been around since 1796 in Naples, Italy and is an all time favorite. Your kids are guaranteed to love it. Prepare for a second order!

Calabrian Soppressata & Olives $8.50

If your kids love spicy food, then they are going to love the Calabrian Soppressata, a salami typical from Calabria, in the South of Italy. We added Mediterranean black olives from the same region to create a unique flavor.

Sausage & Olives $10.50

We use different styles of imported italian sausages for this pizza. Ask your server for the available options, as they change every week. Olives are from the region of Calbria in Italy.

Mushroom & Cherry Tomatoes $9.00

Porcini mushrooms in a combination with mozzarella, olive oil, and arugula or fresh basil. Also available with prosciuto cotto.

Pepperoni Classical $8.00

It’s not a typo. Chef Mario considers himself an artist! Our pizzas are as classical as Giuseppe Verdi’s music. And we got you covered all the four seasons. You are welcome!

Quattro Formaggi $10.00

The four cheese pizza is a delight and is currently the most ordered pizza in our restaurant. It's a safe choice for everyone! Enjoy the combination of mozzarella, bocconcini, parmesan cheese, and romano cheese.
