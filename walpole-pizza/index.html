About us:

We bring the best Italian pizzas in Walpole and Boston area. Giuseppe, the owner, has a clear goal: "The best pizza or nothing". He uses only original recipes passed over from one generation to the other in his family, which owns 2 restaurants in Italy.

Last year, chef Mario Muzzo from Pisa, Italy, joined Walpole-Pizza and since then, the daily visitors have trippled in number. He is the perfect match for Giuseppe's family recipes.

To create unique flavors, in addition to our local farm, we import some key ingredients from Italy.
